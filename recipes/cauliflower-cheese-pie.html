Cauliflower Cheese Pie

A delectable dish made of cauliflower and cheese that serves up to 4 people

This cauliflower cheese pie is made with a delicious potato crust that has Cheddar cheese paired with a lovely sautéed mixture of grated cauliflower, onion, garlic, thyme, and a generous sprinkling of paprika.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups shredded potatoes
  • ¼ cup grated onion
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon all-purpose flour
  • 1 ½ tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • ½ teaspoon dried basil
  • ½ teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 head cauliflower, coarsely chopped
  • 1 ½ cups shredded Cheddar cheese
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • ¼ cup milk
  • ¼ teaspoon paprika
